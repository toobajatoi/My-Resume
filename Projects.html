Laser Alarming System

  • The system comprises components such as an LDR, Transistor CL1100, 5V Buzzer, 9V Power Supply, LED Bulb, Laser Light, Breadboard, and Resistors, all interconnected with jumper wires.
  • When the laser beam is disrupted, the buzzer activates instantly, providing a swift response to potential security breaches. This advancement in security technology offers an affordable yet highly efficient means of safeguarding valuables, from household items to critical organizational assets.
